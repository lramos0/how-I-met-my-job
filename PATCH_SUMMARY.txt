HiringCafe patch v3

Fixes in this bundle:
1. Restored navigation for Company forums.
   - #forums now hides the jobs page and shows #forumView.
   - Jobs tab switches back to the jobs page.
   - Active tab state is updated.
   - Old #f links still redirect to #forums.

2. Fixed the dead hamburger button.
   - The top-right menu now opens a drawer with Jobs, Company forums, Account/Profile, AI Search, and filters shortcuts.

3. Fixed AI Search.
   - The AI Search button now redirects to https://mewannajob.com.

4. Preserved local profile/forum state.
   - Local/Firebase profile state remains initialized by app.js.
   - Forum posts/upvotes remain tied to HiringCafeAuth state.

5. Kept the CORS-safe Job Data Pool proxy approach.
   - /api/jobs remains the browser endpoint.
   - Added a Netlify Function at netlify/functions/jobs.js.
   - netlify.toml now rewrites /api/jobs to the Netlify function.
   - Vercel-style api/jobs.js is still included for Vercel deployments.

What went wrong in the previous bundle:
- The older forum implementation had hash routing for #forums and #company/... routes.
- I replaced it with the local profile/post tracking version but did not wire app.js to unhide #forumView when the Company forums tab was clicked.
- The hamburger and AI Search buttons existed in HTML but had no click handlers.
