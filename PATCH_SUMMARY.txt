HiringCafe patch summary

Changed files:
- app.js
- lib/fortune500.js
- forum.js
- service-worker.js

What changed:
1. Job loading now merges the first 4,000 bundled/local jobs with 4 remote Job Data Pool batches of 500 each, targeting 6,000 total loaded listings after dedupe.
2. Job Data Pool requests now include limit=500 plus offset/page per batch, with GET first and POST fallback.
3. Company logos no longer use Clearbit. Job cards and Fortune helpers now use Google favicon-based logos with inline SVG initials fallback.
4. Homepage count display now shows a cosmetic network-style counter starting around 3,100,000 jobs and 5,600,000 companies, while still including the real loaded filtered count.
5. Added service-worker.js to retain/update the cosmetic counter state.
6. Local account creation now initializes a persistent uid and editable profile data.
7. Account panel now shows display name/headline plus saved-job, post, and upvote stats.
8. Added a forum implementation that tracks posts and per-user upvotes in local state, with Firebase-compatible save hooks through app.js.
