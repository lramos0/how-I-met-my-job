This roadmap frames mewannajob.com as a product that turns raw job and resume activity into timestamped, queryable data for users to conduct analysis. The strategy starts with trustworthy data foundations, then layers in snapshots, analytics, redacted.

Timeline
Phase 01

Brick Laying

Weeks 1–4

Rebuild the persistence layer for jobs and resumes so the system can serve efficient, permissioned, timestamp-aware data retrieval. This phase is the infrastructure bet that enables every downstream analytics feature.

Primary goals

  • Refactor jobs and resumes tables to support time-bounded querying and snapshot generation.
  • Represent both data families as durable objects with timestamps and related metadata.
  • Design permission-aware access patterns so future free and paid datasets can be gated cleanly.
  • Support "glimpses" between date ranges such as 2026-02-01 to 2026-02-28.

Deliverables

  • Schema changes for temporal records
  • Snapshot eligibility rules
  • Metadata normalization layer
  • Access-control strategy for dataset tiers
Temporal querying Permissioned access Snapshot-ready storage
Phase 02

Snapshot Engine

Weeks 5–7

Turn the new data model into a reliable snapshot pipeline. Snapshots are cleaned, time-specific subsets of the underlying data, prepared for exploration and publication. These datasets become both product assets and evidence inside content.

Snapshot behavior

  • Generate subsets for tightly defined date ranges.
  • Remove duplicates, anomalies, and sensitive information before distribution.
  • Package resumes, job listings, and metadata into a browser-consumable format.
  • Make snapshots downloadable and loadable into the analytics experience.

Launch assumptions

  • Free users access article-linked evidence snapshots.
  • Paid users gain access to recurring monthly snapshots.
  • Snapshot docs define provenance, date range, and coverage.
Clean-room datasets SEO evidence payloads Monthly premium archives
Phase 03

Article-to-Data Experience

Weeks 8–9

Create the product loop between editorial content and analytics. Articles produced by the SEO article generator should not just cite trends, but expose the exact snapshot evidence behind those claims.

User experience

  • Readers click through from article evidence to the matching dataset.
  • Datasets can be downloaded or opened immediately in-browser.
  • Free experiences emphasize discoverability and trust through transparent evidence.

Product impact

  • Turns content into a data acquisition funnel.
  • Establishes mewannajob.com as both publisher and analytics source.
  • Builds habit around interacting with structured labor-market evidence.
Content flywheel Evidence UX Trust by transparency

Phase 07

Production Rollout & Monetization

Weeks 19–20

Formalize the analytics-first launch. Free users get curated evidence snapshots attached to published articles, while paid users get broader recurring access to monthly datasets and the deeper exploratory workflow.

Free tier

  • Access to select snapshots used as evidence in SEO-generated articles.
  • Enough functionality to demonstrate credibility and product value.

Paid tier

  • Monthly snapshot access from the start.
  • Deeper use of the analytics workflow and visual tools.
  • Foundation for future premium data products.
