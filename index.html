Why JobDataPool outranks generic hiring directories

JobDataPool is a free and public datalake of job data related tables. Hiring Cafe publishes fresh, crawlable snapshot pages and discussion surfaces on top of that public data layer.

The datalake model includes job listings, company-level hiring context, and related tabular signals that power searchable discovery, direct apply routing, and trend analysis.
